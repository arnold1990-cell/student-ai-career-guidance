# Edutech Product Overview

## Executive Summary

Edutech is an AI student career guidance platform that connects learners with suitable career pathways and bursary opportunities while enabling companies and administrators to manage programs, applications, and engagement at scale.

## Objectives

- Improve career decision quality for students through AI-assisted recommendations.
- Increase access to bursary and funding opportunities.
- Provide organizations with a channel to discover and support future talent.
- Offer administrators full operational oversight, governance, and analytics.

## Core Roles

### Student

- Creates a profile with academic history, interests, and goals.
- Completes career assessments and receives recommendations.
- Browses and applies for bursaries and opportunities.
- Tracks application statuses and notification updates.

### Company

- Publishes bursaries, scholarships, internships, and programs.
- Defines candidate criteria and manages submissions.
- Reviews applicant pipelines and communicates with candidates.

### Admin

- Manages users, roles, and platform access.
- Oversees data quality, moderation, and compliance.
- Configures recommendation parameters and system settings.
- Monitors subscriptions, payments, and operational reports.

## Functional Modules

- Identity and access management (role-based)
- Student profile and assessment module
- AI recommendation engine (career + bursary)
- Bursary and opportunity marketplace
- Application workflow and status tracking
- Company management and review tools
- Admin console and reporting dashboards
- Subscription and payment processing
- Notification and communication services

## Integrations

- AI/LLM services for recommendation generation
- Payment gateway for subscription billing
- Email/SMS/push notification providers
- Optional school or LMS data import connectors

## Assumptions

- Internet connectivity is available for cloud-based AI services.
- Data privacy and consent requirements are enforced at onboarding.
- Company partners maintain current and accurate opportunity listings.
- Initial release targets a web platform, with mobile expansion planned.
